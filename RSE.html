MAEMOD Research Software Engineering

- Develop and Maintain Robust Mathematical Models: A key part of our team's role are to create, enhance, and maintain mathematical models to simulate the spread of infectious diseases.

- Foster Cross-Disciplinary Collaboration: Encourage collaboration between the research software engineering team, the mathematical modeling team, and other relevant teams such as epidemiologists or biostatisticians.

- Continuously Improve and Update Tools and Techniques: Keep up with the latest advancements in software engineering, mathematical modeling, and tropical medicine. Implement these advancements into our works to ensure that our team remains at the cutting edge of the field.

- Promote Open Science and Reproducibility: Ensure that all code and models produced are well-documented and reproducible.

- Support Grant Applications and Fundraising: Collaborate with other members of the research unit to write technical sections of grant applications, demonstrating the value of software and models developed by MORU.

- User Training and Support: Provide training and ongoing support for users of software developed/applied within the research unit.
